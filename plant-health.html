Photography Tips for Better Analysis

Good Lighting

Take photos in natural daylight or bright, even lighting conditions.

Clear Focus

Ensure the affected area is in sharp focus and clearly visible.

Close-up View

Steady Shot

Keep your hands steady to avoid blurry images.

Common Plant Health Issues

Pest Damage

Insects and other pests can cause visible damage to leaves, stems, and fruits.

Fungal Diseases

Spots, mold, and discoloration often indicate fungal infections.

Nutrient Deficiency

Yellowing, browning, or unusual leaf coloration may indicate nutrient problems.

Environmental Stress

Heat, cold, drought, or overwatering can cause various symptoms.

Project Hardware

The core components powering our IoT agricultural robot

Sensors & Components

DHT22 Sensor

Measures ambient temperature and humidity to provide crucial environmental data for crop health monitoring.

Ultrasonic Distance Sensors

Two sensors (front and back) for obstacle detection and autonomous navigation, ensuring the robot moves safely.

Soil Moisture Sensor

Provides real-time data on soil hydration levels, enabling the smart irrigation system to water crops precisely when needed.

Water Level Sensor

Monitors the water level in the irrigation tank, ensuring the robot has an adequate water supply for its tasks.

Chassis & Propulsion

Robot Frame & Motors

The robot's frame is constructed from durable cardboard. It is powered by four 60 RPM BO motors, providing stable front and back movement. The robot is controlled via a joystick module, allowing for manual operation and testing.

Servo Motors & Actuators

Multiple servo motors are used for precise control of components:

  • Camera Mount: One servo for panning the embedded camera.
  • Distance Sensors: Two servos for positioning the front and back sensors.
  • Water Level Sensor: One servo to adjust the sensor's position.
  • DHT22 Sensor: One servo for optimal placement of the environmental sensor.

Why Weather Matters in Agriculture

Irrigation Planning

Monitor precipitation and humidity to optimize watering schedules and reduce water waste.

Temperature Monitoring

Wind Conditions

Visibility

Smart Farming Tips

Morning Check: Check weather conditions every morning to plan your daily farming activities.

Weekly Planning: Use 7-day forecasts to plan irrigation, fertilization, and harvesting schedules.

Storm Preparation: Monitor severe weather alerts to protect crops and equipment in advance.

Humidity Tracking: High humidity can increase disease risk - adjust preventive treatments accordingly.
